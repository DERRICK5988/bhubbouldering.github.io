Bouldering

Bouldering ボルダリング is a type of rock climbing where climbers ascend short, challenging routes
(called "problems") without ropes. It's typically done on artificial walls or outdoor
boulders, focusing on strength, technique, and problem-solving skills. Climbers use
climbing shoes and chalk for grip. Bouldering is known for powerful and dynamic
movements and is graded by difficulty. It's a popular and accessible form of climbing,
providing a great workout and fostering a sense of community among climbers.
